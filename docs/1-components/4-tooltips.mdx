# Tooltips

## Tooltip Example

Tooltips are relatively simple as of now. They take in three props:

1. a `title`, the content of the tooltip
1. a `position`
1. a render prop `children`

<TooltipExample />

Hacker UI's tooltips don't add anything to the DOM until you hover over them. They use <Anchor href="https://reactjs.org/docs/portals.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">React DOM portals</Anchor> internally.

You can add a Tooltip to any element that takes in a `ref` to a DOM element.
